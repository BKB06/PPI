Introduction

   This website is the result of an Integrated Professional Practice (PPI) project from the Instituto Federal Farroupilha, Santo Augusto campus, developed in collaboration between the subjects of English, Programming, and Geography. The aim of the project is to integrate language learning with technology and geographical context.

   As you explore this site, you will find resources that demonstrate how language learning can be enhanced through the use of digital tools. It serves as an example of how education can be innovative and dynamic, combining different fields of study to create a broader and more applied understanding of knowledge.

   Duolingo is a popular language-learning platform with 31.4 million active users worldwide. It offers a robust free version, which includes access to a variety of language courses with interactive and fun lessons. Additionally, there is a paid version, Duolingo Plus, which removes ads and provides some extra features, such as unlimited lives and personalized progress tracking. This makes Duolingo an accessible and effective tool for both beginners and those looking to enhance their language skills.

Tutorial

  1. Access the Website or App
    • Open your internet browser and type https://duolingo.com/ in the address bar.
    • If you prefer using your mobile device, download the Duolingo app from the App Store (iOS) or Google Play(Android).
  2. Start the Registration Process
    • Upon accessing the site or opening the app, you'll see the option "Get Started" or "Log In."
    • Click on "Get Started" to create a new account.
  3. Choose a Language to Learn
    • On the next screen, you'll be prompted to select the language you want to learn.
    • Choose your preferred language from the list.
  4. Set a Daily Goal
    • Duolingo offers the option to set a daily learning goal.
    • Choose the goal that best suits your pace (Casual, Regular, Serious, or Insane).
    • This goal can be adjusted later in the settings if needed.
  5. Create an Account
    • With Email: Enter your email address, create a username, and set a password. Make sure to use a valid email.
    • With Facebook: Click on "Continue with Facebook" to use your Facebook account for registration.
    • With Google: Click on "Continue with Google" to use your Google account.
  6. Verify Your Account
    • If you signed up using your email, Duolingo will send a verification email.
    • Check your email, find the verification message from Duolingo, and click the provided link to activate your account.
  7. Set Up Your Profile (Optional)
    • After verification, you can personalize your profile by adding a photo and personal information.
    • This helps customize your experience on Duolingo.
  8. Start Learning
    • With your registration complete, you're ready to begin your language lessons.
    • Follow the on-screen instructions to start your first lesson.
